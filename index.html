About Us

Free Building Trading Est.

Free Building Trade International Co. (FBT) was established in 1996, having its main offices in the United States, Hong Kong, China, Jordan and also representatives in many countries all over the world. FBT believes in making markets having the utmost possibility to reach it and providing knowledge, value and services which the customer deserves..

FBT conducts businesses all over the world based on quality and post-sale services which enable us to build strong relationships on the basis of trust with our customers. We are keen on providing customers with the highest quality services and products with the best prices.

The main activity of the FBT is the sanitary materials and accessories. We have the productivity with great quantities meeting all customer orders in the mentioned above fields, in addition to the quick execution of orders and possibilities of shipping and carriage to your destinations. For more details, visit the website.

The FBT has sections specialized in sales and commercial services through our branches in China, which are represented in (building and decoration materials, electrical materials, electronics, equipment, machinery and production lines…etc.)
